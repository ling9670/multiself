01 — The Idea

A person is not one thing.

Multiself gives every facet of a person its own card — the cat sitter, the farmer, the artist — so each role is met on its own terms, yet clearly belongs to the same hand.

The product is a set of collectible character cards for real people. Where a game introduces a hero with a name, a portrait and a set of abilities, Multiself introduces a self: a role someone genuinely lives, with its own work, story, offerings and ways to connect. The cards link in a single timeline, so moving between selves feels like turning the pages of one collection.

Many, not one

We never flatten a person into a single title. Each self is whole, distinct, and worthy of a full page.

Seen, not sold

Quiet confidence over hype. We present a person with the restraint of a fine portrait, not a billboard.

One hand, one thread

Distinct colours and treatments per self, unified by type, paper and a single timeline that ties them together.

03 — Colour

Muted jewel tones on warm ivory.

A warm-neutral foundation — ivory paper, warm near-black ink, champagne gold — holds the system together. Each self is then assigned one muted jewel tone: same lightness and chroma, only the hue shifts. This keeps every role equal in weight while unmistakably its own. New selves extend the set by choosing a new hue at the same oklch(0.55 0.08 H) recipe.

05 — The Three Templates

One concept, three expressions.

Every self can be presented in one of three card templates. The content is identical — only the personality of the frame changes. A client chooses the template that suits their tone; the timeline, palette and type stay constant across all three.

06 — Card Anatomy

What every self contains.

Regardless of template, a self is one scrolling page built from the same ordered parts. Keep the order; the rhythm is part of the brand.

01

The Header

Portrait, person name, role and a one-line tagline. The self's colour saturates this zone.

02

The Attributes

Established year, location and type, plus three signature traits rated in dots. This replaces any “difficulty” or game score — it introduces a person, not a level.

03

The Story

A lead line in italic serif, then a short paragraph. First person, warm, never a résumé.

04

Past Work

A gallery — one feature image and a supporting pair — with a quiet mono caption.

05

Video

One feature clip plus two shorts. Show the self in motion.

06

Shop & Offerings

Products or services with price and a single buy / book / enquire action each.

07

Connect

Links shown as icon tiles — the glyph names the platform; no text label needed.

08

Invitation

A closing line — “Every self deserves its own card” — and a single call to create your own multiself.

07 — Connective Tissue

The thread between selves.

A slim timeline rests at the foot of every card. It names the current self, marks each self as a node in its own colour, and lets you swipe between them. It is quiet by design — a translucent ivory bar, never a heavy control — because the selves are the subject, not the navigation.

08 — Tone of Voice

Warm, first-person, unhurried.
