How it works

Three steps.
One complete you.

01

Create your profile

Pick a username. Your profile lives at multiself.com/you. Takes two minutes — no design skills, no code, no blank page staring back at you.

02

Add your identities

Each identity gets its own page — a name, an image, a description, your links. Start with three. Add more as you grow. Each one is a different door into the same person.

03

Share one link

Put it in your Instagram bio, your email signature, your Slack profile. One link. Every version of you. Let people find the part of you that's right for them.
